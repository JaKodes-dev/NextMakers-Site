ABOUT NextMakers FOUNDATION

NextMakers Foundation is a youth-focused organization dedicated to empowering the next generation of innovators, entrepreneurs,and leaders through practical skills training, mentorship, and collaborative opportunities.The Foundation builds a community of young change-makers who develop real-world solutions,launch startups, and contribute to economic development.


MISSION

"To empower young people with practical skills, entrepreneurial mindset, and collaborative opportunities that enable them to create innovative solutions and sustainable impact in their communities."


VISION

To become a leading global platform where young innovators, entrepreneurs, and problem-solvers develop the skills and network needed to build the future.

CORE VALUES

  • Innovation:Encouraging creativity and problem solving.
  • Empowerment:Equipping young people with skills and opportunities.
  • Collaboration:Building strong communities and partnerships.
  • Impact:Creating measurable social and economic change.
  • Inclusivity:Providing oppoertunities for diverse communities.

ORGANIZATION STRUCTURE

Board of Directors

Provides strategic oversight and governance.

Executive Leadership

Leads day-to-day operations and program implementation.

Departments

Oversees specific programs and initiatives.

Volunteer Work

Provides guidance, support, and expertise to participants.
